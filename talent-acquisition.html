Search Methodology

Keeping in mind that every search is unique and every client has different needs, we tailor to each search and add consultative value whenever possible. With our Consultants’ industry knowledge and experience, as well as the ability to understand chemistry, we work towards a cultural “fit” between the candidate and the company.

Our USP (Unique Search Philosophy) is a STEP-by-STEP process:

  • Step 1: Define Objectives and Specifications
  • Step 2: Identification of Target Organizations
  • Step 3: Resume Mining
  • Step 4: Identify and assess candidates
  • Step 5: Profile/Resume Presentation
  • Step 6: Organizing Interviews
  • Step 7: Assisting in closure
  • Step 8: Reference Check
  • Step 9: Ensuring candidate joining/delivery
  • Step 10: Regular feedback from Client
